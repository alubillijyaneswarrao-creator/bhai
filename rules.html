Rules & Regulations

General Conduct

  • All participants must exhibit good sportsmanship at all times.
  • Follow all instructions from judges and event staff immediately.
  • Participants are responsible for their own safety and the safety of those around them. This means using equipment as instructed, managing your weights responsibly (e.g., using collars, not dropping empty barbells), and being aware of your surroundings. If you are injured or feel unwell, you must stop competing and notify a staff member immediately
  • The use of any illegal or performance-enhancing drugs is strictly forbidden. The event organizers reserve the right to test any athlete at any time. A positive test will result in a ban from all future competitions and stripping of any titles or prizes won.
  • All equipment must be treated with care and returned to its designated place after use, unless otherwise instructed by a judge. Deliberately damaging equipment or leaving your area in a mess can result in penalties or disqualification

Registration

  • Registrations are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Participants must provide valid identification (Aadhar card) during registration.
  • All registration payments are subject to verification by the RAW organizing team. Any participant found to have engaged in fraudulent activities, including but not limited to, submitting a fake, altered, or manipulated payment screenshot, will be immediately disqualified from the current event without a refund.
  • The individual will be banned from participating in any and all subsequent events within the current RAW season. The organizers reserve the right to take further action as deemed necessary.
